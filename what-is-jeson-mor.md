# What is Jeson Mor?

[Jeosn Mor](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jeson_Mor "Jeson Mor on Wikipedia") is a board strategy game considered to be a [variant of Chess](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_chess_variants "Chess variants on Wikipedia"). It is based on chess as it includes some of its concepts in the game, however the rules are much simpler and involving fewer components in one match.
